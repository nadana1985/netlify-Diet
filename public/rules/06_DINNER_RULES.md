# DINNER RULES (LOCKED)

Primary dinner pattern:
- Vegetable-specific soup
- Raw cucumber salad

Carbohydrate-inclusive dinners:
- Idli: exactly 2 days per week
- Chapati: exactly 1 day per week

On Idli / Chapati days:
- Vegetable-specific soup is sufficient
- NO separate vegetable curry is required
- Raw cucumber salad is mandatory

Explicitly disallowed:
- Rice at dinner
- Non-veg at dinner
