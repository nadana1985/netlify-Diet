# LUNCH RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Every lunch MUST include:
- Rice

Additionally, ONLY ONE of the following:
- One cooked vegetable dish
OR
- One non-veg dish

Allowed additions:
- Rasam
- Sambar
- Fish kuzhambu
- Raw cucumber salad (optional)

Explicitly disallowed:
- Skipping lunch
- Second cooked vegetable
