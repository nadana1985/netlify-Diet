# NON-VEG RULESET

Allowed items:
- Chicken (air-fried or pepper fry)
- Fish (fish kuzhambu only)
- Egg (boiled whole egg only, optional)

Frequency:
- Chicken: maximum 2 days per week
- Fish: maximum 2 days per week
- Egg: optional, up to 2 days per week

Sequencing rule (HARD):
- NO consecutive non-veg days
- Every non-veg day MUST be followed by a veg-only day
